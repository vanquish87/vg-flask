About Business :

Transportation is a key pillar for any economy to grow n sustain & when we talk about India with vast Highways & Train track network we should not forget the geography of India. We have such a long & beautiful coast-line not to mention a location on Globe which connects Eastern globe to western counter-part.

This geography has created another line of Monopolistic line of business with longevity which is Coastal Shipping Transportation.

Today I bring to you Shreyas Shipping & Logistics which handles almost 60% of India's Container Cargo & has a scalable business model.

Shreyas promoter understands business very well & I find them focussed towards creating wealth for shareholders.

These guys are constantly upping their game as they recently bought 3 more Vessels (Ships) at Kude ke bhaav using ships ki market ki mandi & increased their capacity.

SWOT Analysis :

Strengths

  • 9 Vessels with average age of 20 years.
  • Shreyas handles almost 60% of India's Container Cargo .
  • Dominance in Western Coastline (WCC and PIX-1).
  • Remuneration policy of Shreyas Shipping & Logistics are investor friendly & promoter seems to be working in favour of Shareholders.
  • Shreyas has replaced two vessels with higher capacity n added three new vessels when prices of newer vessels are at historic low levels. This shows the intention to scale & smartness of promoter & promoter group.

weaknesses

  • Efforts needed to gain business on east coastline.
  • Dry docking period of 15-30 days needed to put each vessel on rest & manage risk which gives somewhat inconsistency in business.

opportunities

  • Coastal shipping presently accounts for only 7% of overall cargo movement in India.
  • Coastal shipping is cheaper & scalable way of cargo movement which needs to be promoted & is being promoted by govt. of India as we speak.

threats

  • Sharp fluctuations inr-dollar rate is always a risk.
  • A major threat being availability of adequate bunkering facilities.
  • Inadequate bunkering infrastructure at most minor/intermediate ports acts as an hindrance to coastal shipping.


Business Verdict :

The promoter is now aggressive with expansions but Howe Robinson Container Index is flat to downward & hence cargo shipment charges are at very low level. I expect this condition to recover as Howe Robinson Container Index being cyclical in nature & we are at almost bottom of cycle.

Shreyas Shipping & Logistics is a Multibagger stock in India available undervalued. ValueGuyz this stock is a definite premium value pick to consider for your research.
