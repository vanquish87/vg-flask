Acrylic Fibre is mainly used for clothes and Acrylic Yarn produced from Acrylic Fibre is warm to manufacture Sweaters, Scarves,T-Shirts, Kids Clothes, Mufflers, Jogging Suits, Socks etc.

Our Pick ie, Pasupati Acrylon is a B2B (Business to business) company manufacturing AF ie, acrylic fibres.

Pasupati Acrylon has a plant in Moradabad (UP) area where it has installed capacity of more than 40,000 TPA of AFs


SWOT Analysis :

Strengths

  • Acrylic Fibre by nature is a high margin business and Pasupati has its all turnover coming from it till date.
  • Pasupati Acrylon commenced production of Cast Polypropylene Film (CPP) from 1st September, 2017.
  • With shareholders approval last year Mr. Jain ie, promoter of Pasupati Acrylon has started diversifying with CPP 1st in line.
  • CPP films are transparent cast polypropylene films designed to offer high performance, great appearance and easy converting for flexible packaging and other applications.
  • CPP is growing in popularity among many areas as a more attractive packaging material against polyethylene. It is again a good margin product and would add significant profits into Pasupati Acrylon.
  • Pasupati increased production of AF well in advance of winters. With this year's winters coming in early would ensure higher turnover as well as profits for the business.
  • Promoter is inclined towards value creations & business has excellent remuneration policies. I am happy to mention that they are very welcoming to analysts.

weaknesses

  • Not tied up to big brands likes of Shoppers Stop, Westside where demand is heavy and consistent.
  • Pasupati Acrylon is not inclined towards R&D and are at mercy of others innovation to take industry forward. This doesn't look good for very long term view.

opportunities

  • Major raw material in AF production is ACN (AcryloNitrile) which is a by-product of Crude oil. Lower stable prices of Crude can help AF industry to innovate low cost solutions for carpet, blanket industry.

threats

  • Warm weather forecasts for winters poses a huge threat for production of Acrylic fibres.
  • Pasupati Acrylon exports 30% + of its production. Sharp fluctuations inr-dollar rate is always a risk.
  • Commissioning of AF manufacturers of China in 2016 has raised a threat on exports since it raised competition in international markets.
  • Sharp volatility in crude oil prices poses quite a problem in importing raw material ie ACN (AcryloNitrile).


Business Verdict :

Being a Lala company at heart, Pasupati Acrylon has recorded strong growth in past and passion of Mr. Vineet Jain ie, promoter for business is admirable.

I am very optimistic with nature of working, expansion into CPP of Pasupati. These guys are working in manufacturing of high margin products ie, AF and recently CPP.

Even in worst case scenario Pasupati Acrylon is bound to increase turnover and profitability over coming years.

Pasupati Acrylon Ltd is a Multibagger stock in India with unique products ie, Acrylic fibres (AF) and Cast Polypropylene Film (CPP). ValueGuyz this stock is a definite premium value pick to consider for your research.
